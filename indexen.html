Resume

Resume

Sissi Fleitas is a renowned Cuban-American personality followed by hundreds of thousands on Instagram. Her career began on stage at age four and took off on TV at fifteen with Cubavisión and later Univisión’s “Sábado Gigante.” As an actress, host and communicator, she’s starred in hit telenovelas, anchored the Spanish version of “The Tonight Show,” and appeared at major international festivals. She made her film debut in 2010 and starred in the Cannes-selected feature “Rumble.” A cover girl for magazines like Maxim and People en Español, she founded the beauty line ElyssBel by Sissi Fleitas, has developed real estate ventures, serves as a global brand ambassador and actively supports philanthropic initiatives across Latin America and the U.S.
